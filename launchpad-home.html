The fundraising space is broken. We’ve come a long way, but we can do better. Today’s decentralized platforms are slow, confusing, expensive, and isolate both new and experienced investors. Participation is restricted to a select few and technical issues seem to be the norm, rather than the exception.

Blockchain innovations are accelerating faster every day, it’s time for fundraising to catch up.

1. Sign Up and KYC

In order to participate in sales on EduLaunch, you must sign up and KYC first. You can still stake and earn EDUM without registering.

2. Verify Wallet

Once you have registered and submitted your KYC, you must verify your wallet. This is the only wallet you will be able to use for sales.

3. Stake EDUM

By staking EDUM, you earn allocation in IDOs. If you do not want to participate in sales, you can still benefit from staking.

4. Register for Sale

During the registration period, you must confirm your interest in participation. Once registration closes, you will not be able to register until the next sale.
